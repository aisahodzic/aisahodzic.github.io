Did you hear the rain?

The Magic of Rainmaking

The Magic of Rainmaking project is a website which brings an experience of the rainmaking rituals of tribes from different parts of the world. The aim of the project is to raise awareness of tribal practices and contribute to the reconnection of humans to nature, in a time where technology became a dominant factor of everyday life. We wanted to educate you about the tribes Lakota, Dongria and Australian Aborigines, through simulating their successful rainmaking singing ritual followed by the rainfall. You can combine provided individual sounds in order to make your own rain melody, and thus participate in the simulation of natural phenomena. This website offers an experience of enjoying and immersing oneself into the beauties of nature and rain, while being knowledgeable of the ritual practices, present at the moment of rituals and participating in the rain sound-making process in its own way. This one is for all rain lovers.
