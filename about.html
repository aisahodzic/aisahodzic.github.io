About 30MFF and New Friends

About New Friends


The scenario for New Friends is inspired by the on-going isolation process and pandemic measures in which individuals are encouraged to avoid gatherings and stay inside. Like many of their friends, Matthew, Saf, Hasin and Aisha have experienced life on NYUAD campus during the pandemic. The mandatory measures are contributing to the health and well-being of the campus community, but they affect each individual in a different way.

New Friends encourages the conversation on the challenges of the “new normal”, especially among the student population.

About 30MFF


The 30-Minute Film Festival is a festival organized by the CommLab course in New York University Abu Dhabi. The festival consists of a number of films, and all actors starring in the films are students who are taking the CommLab course. All films are directed and edited by the students.

Due to Covid-19, students are still scattered all over the world. This unique situation made the film-making process challenging, yet students managed to come up with amazing and creative art pieces. The premieres of all films for the course members were on the 9th of September 2020. The films are available for the public from 29th September 2020.
