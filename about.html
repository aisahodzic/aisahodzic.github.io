About New Friends


        The scenario for New Friends is inspired by the on-going isolation process and pandemic measures in which individuals are encouraged to avoid gatherings and stay inside. The mandatory measures are contributing to the health and well-being of the campus community, yet they affect each individual in a different way.

Like many of their friends, Matthew, Saf, Hasin and Aisha have experienced life on NYUAD campus during the pandemic. They decided to turn their isolation story into a movie to which other students can relate.

New Friends encourages the conversation on the challenges of the “new normal”, especially among the student population.

About 30MFF


         The 30-Minute Film Festival is a festival organized by the CommLab course in New York University Abu Dhabi. The festival consists of a number of films, and all directiors, tech staff, and actors starring in the films are students who are taking the CommLab course.

Due to Covid-19, students are still scattered all over the world. This unique situation made the film-making process challenging, yet students managed to come up with amazing and creative art pieces. The premieres of all films for the course members were on the 9th of September 2020. The films are available for the public from 29th September 2020.
